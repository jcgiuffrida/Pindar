Smarter than the others.

We know quotes are more than “quotables,” and should be read in context. That’s why we use machine-learning techniques to show you quotes that relate to each other, such as allusions and translations, and quotes that other readers also liked. This keeps popular quotes from drowning out literary ones or academic ones, and vice versa—so everyone is satisfied. We also know that not all quotes are equal—and let you include your favorite song lyrics, poetry, and religious verses. (Links between quotes are not yet functional)
